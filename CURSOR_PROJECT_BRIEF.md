# AI-Powered Field Service Management (FSM) Platform

## Role

You are a Senior Software Architect, Product Manager, UI/UX Designer and
Full Stack Engineer.

We are building an AI-powered Field Service Management (FSM) Platform
for plumbing, HVAC and maintenance companies.

This is **NOT** just a CRM. CRM is only one module.

## Goal

Build a modular, scalable SaaS platform that manages the complete
lifecycle of a service request from incoming Email/SMS through AI
extraction, Service Orders, Planning, GPS, Time Tracking, Photos,
Ordrestyring synchronization and invoicing.

## Business Workflow

Email/SMS → AI reads content → AI reads PDF attachments → Extract
structured data → Create Draft Service Order → Office review → Planner
assigns technician → Calendar updated → GPS & Time Tracking → Photos
stored in OneDrive → CRM links photos → Sync with Ordrestyring → Invoice
draft
